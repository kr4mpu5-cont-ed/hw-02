About Me

Hello. I am an experienced technology leader, strategic thinker, problem solver, and lifelong learner. I believe that combining technology with good design, strategy, and business sense can benefit real people, that human-centric, cross-discipline teams produce the most desirable and sustainable outcomes.

In that time, I and my teams have developed innovative technology solutions for numerous industries including food and beverage, insurance, consumer products,transportation, mobile and enterprise communications, entertainment, healthcare, and transportation, among others.
